How to Play

Start Your Sea Journey
Draw and match cards to clear the deck while unlocking new mystical treasures as you progress.
Every card has a unique oceanic design, and the deeper you go, the more powerful the magic!
Match Cards and Unleash Power
Match cards of the same rank or suit to clear them from the deck and open up new pathways to your next treasure.
Unlock Hidden Relics
As you complete levels, special maps and relics will unlock, revealing hidden treasures that provide special abilities, such as extra moves or magical powers to help you clear the deck faster.
Navigate Through Storms
Sometimes, the ocean will test your strategy with storm cards—these add complexity and mystery to each level, making you think twice before making your move.
Collect and Upgrades
The more you explore, the more powerful your cards become. Upgrade your deck with magical abilities to help you through the toughest levels and reveal the secrets hidden beneath the sea.
